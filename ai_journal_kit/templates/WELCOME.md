## Your Journal Structure

These folders were created for you:

- `daily/` - Your daily notes
- `projects/` - Active projects
- `areas/` - Life domains (health, work, etc.)
- `resources/` - Reference materials
- `people/` - Relationship notes
- `memories/` - Captured insights & breakthroughs
- `archive/` - Completed items

You don't need to use all of them! Start with just daily notes and let it evolve.
