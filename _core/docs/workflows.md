# Common Workflows

Practical workflows for using your AI-powered markdown journal.

## Daily Workflows

**1. Open your journal**
- Navigate to `daily/` folder

**2. Start AI coach** (optional)
```

AI: [Greets you, asks about sleep, energy, and priorities]
```

**3. Fill in morning sections**
- Energy level (‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê)

- Quick scan of yesterday's note
- Any carry-over items?
- Learnings to apply today?

**Time investment:** 5-10 minutes

### Evening Reflection Routine

**1. Review your day**
- What got done?
- What didn't? (and why?)
- Energy patterns observed

**2. Capture learnings**
- What did I learn today?
- What would I do differently?
- What am I grateful for?

**3. Set tomorrow's priority**
- What's the ONE thing that matters most tomorrow?

**4. Update with AI coach** (optional)
```

AI: [Reviews with you, helps capture insights]
```

**Time investment:** 5-10 minutes

## Weekly Workflows

### Sunday/Monday Weekly Planning

**1. Review last week**
- Scan daily notes from previous week
- What patterns do you notice?
- What worked well? What didn't?

**2. Check on projects**
- Review active projects
- What progress was made?
- What's blocked or stuck?

**3. Review areas**
- Quick check-in on 2-3 priority areas
- Are you maintaining standards?
- What needs attention?

**4. Plan this week**
- What are the big rocks for this week?
- Which projects get focus time?
- What meetings/commitments exist?

**5. With AI coach** (optional)
```
You: "Let's do my weekly review"

AI: [Guides you through reflection and planning]
```

**Time investment:** 20-30 minutes

---

### Mid-Week Check-In (Wednesday)

**Quick pulse check:**
- Am I on track with this week's priorities?
- What's working? What's not?
- Do I need to adjust plans?
- Any obstacles to address?

**Time investment:** 5-10 minutes

---

## Monthly Workflows

### Monthly Review & Planning

**1. Review the month**
- Scan through daily notes
- Review completed projects
- Check progress on areas
- Identify patterns and themes

**2. Areas deep-dive**
- Review all active areas
- Are you meeting standards?
- What needs more attention?
- What's going well?

**3. Projects assessment**
- What got completed?
- What's on track?
- What's stuck? Why?
- Any projects to start/pause/archive?

**4. Update goals**
- Are current goals still relevant?
- Any new goals to add?
- What's working in your approach?

**5. Archive completed items**
- Move finished projects to archive
- Celebrate completions
- Extract learnings

**Time investment:** 45-60 minutes

---

## Project Workflows

### Starting a New Project

**1. Create project note**
- Copy `system/project-template.md`
- Rename: `projects/my-project.md`

**2. Define the project**
- What's the outcome?
- Why does it matter?
- When is it due?

**3. Break it down**
- Phases or milestones
- Key tasks
- Dependencies

**4. Link from areas**
- Which area does this project support?
- Link in the relevant area note

**5. First daily mention**
- Reference in today's daily note
- Block time to work on it

---

### Weekly Project Check-In

**For each active project:**
1. What progress was made?
2. What's the next step?
3. Any obstacles?
4. On track for deadline?

**Update project note:**
- Add progress log entry
- Update task checkboxes
- Adjust timeline if needed

---

### Completing a Project

**1. Mark complete**
- Add completion date
- Check off all tasks

**2. Project retrospective**
```markdown
## Reflection

### What Went Well
- [List successes]

### What Could Be Improved
- [List lessons]

### Key Learnings
- [What you'll apply next time]
```

**3. Archive**
- Move to `archive/projects/YYYY/`
- Link from area if relevant

**4. Celebrate!**
- Add to wins in daily note
- Share with AI coach
- Take a moment to acknowledge

## Learning & Growth Workflows

### Capturing Learning Resources

**When you find something valuable:**
1. Create resource note in `resources/`
2. Capture key takeaways
3. Note how to apply it
4. Link from relevant areas/projects

**Template:**
```markdown
# Resource: [Title]

**Source:** [Link]
**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD

## Key Takeaways
- Main point 1
- Main point 2
- Main point 3

## How to Apply
[Practical application]

## Related
- [[Related project]]
- [[Related area]]
```

**With AI coach:**

You: "I just finished reading [book]. Want to help me process it?"

## Crisis/Overwhelm Workflows

### When You're Overwhelmed

**1. Brain dump**
- Open today's daily note
- Write everything swirling in your head
- Don't organize, just get it out

**2. With AI coach**
```
You: "I'm feeling really overwhelmed right now"

AI: [Helps you process, identify priorities, break things down]
```

**3. Identify the ONE thing**
- What's the single most important thing?
- What's the smallest first step?
- Do just that

**4. Release the rest**
- Everything else can wait
- Focus on the one thing
- Breathe

---

### When You've Fallen Off Track

**1. No judgment**
- It's okay. Everyone falls off sometimes.
- You're here now. That's what matters.

**2. Don't try to catch up**
- Don't backfill all the daily notes
- Start fresh with today
- Look forward, not backward

**3. Quick reset**
```
You: "get the date - it's been 2 weeks"

AI: "Welcome back! No judgment. What's been happening?
     Want to catch up or just start fresh today?"
```

**4. One small step**
- Just do today's morning planning
- That's enough to restart momentum

## Tips for Success

### Start Simple
- Don't try all workflows at once
- Master daily routine first
- Add weekly/monthly gradually
- Customize as you go

### Be Consistent, Not Perfect
- Missing a day is fine
- Brief notes better than no notes
- Progress over perfection
- Build the habit slowly

### Review and Iterate
- Weekly: What's working?
- Monthly: What to adjust?
- Quarterly: Big picture reflection
- Continuously evolve the system

---

**Remember:** These are starting points, not prescriptions. Adapt any workflow to fit your life, schedule, and preferences. The best system is the one you actually use.
